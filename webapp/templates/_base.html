Welcome — quick heads-up before you start.

Every photo you upload, every confirmation you make, and every correction you add becomes training data for the AI. The model gets better at HSE classification over time as you and your colleagues use it.

Your photos and edits stay inside AECIS — they're not shared with third parties beyond the AI providers we use to classify them.

Accurate confirmations help the AI more than passive use. If a prediction is wrong, take a moment to correct it.
